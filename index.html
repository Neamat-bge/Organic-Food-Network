Organic milk refers to a number of milk products from livestock raised according to organic farming methods. In general these regulations stipulate that livestock must be: allowed to graze, be fed an organically certified fodder or compound feed, not be treated with most drugs (including bovine growth hormone, other different types of hormones also found ), and in general must be treated humanely.
